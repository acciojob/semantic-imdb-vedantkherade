Summary

The Dark Knight (2008), directed by Christopher Nolan, follows Batman as he faces his greatest psychological and physical challenges.

The Joker, a chaotic criminal mastermind, pushes Gotham City to the brink, forcing Batman to test his moral limits in order to save the city he swore to protect.

Actors

  • Christian Bale as Bruce Wayne / Batman
  • Heath Ledger as The Joker
  • Aaron Eckhart as Harvey Dent / Two-Face
  • Maggie Gyllenhaal as Rachel Dawes
